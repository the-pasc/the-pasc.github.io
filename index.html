History

The PaSc, originally known as PaSc Clan, was founded in 2021 by a group of anime nerds and gamers. Over the years, it has grown into a large community of people where you can express yourself however you want! Our goal is to make an anime, a series like no other. With our large community by our side we can make that possible.
