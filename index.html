History

The PaSc, formerly known as PaSc Clan, was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate anime fans and gamers. Our journey began with a shared dream: to create an anime series unlike any other. In 2024, we rebranded to *The PaSc*, reflecting our evolution from a clan to a creative collective with big ambitions!

Key Milestones:

  • Founded in 2021
  • Rebranded as "The PaSc" in 2024
  • Expanded into anime production and collaborative projects
  • Community-driven storytelling and character creation
